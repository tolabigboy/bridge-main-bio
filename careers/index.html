careers

We take pride in employing the industry’s brightest minds, who share our mission to discover, create, test, and deliver transformative medicines to treat patients who suffer from genetic diseases and cancers with clear genetic drivers.

We’re always on the lookout for individuals whose passion for creating life-changing medicines will inspire hands-on engagement and the dogged pursuit of novel solutions in the face of adversity. Join the BridgeBio team!

Notice To Applicants:

Please be aware of the potential for fraud or scams from individuals, organizations, and internet sites that claim to represent BridgeBio Pharma in recruitment activities. BridgeBio follows a formal and established recruitment process for all authorized, posted positions. This process requires interviews conducted live with BridgeBio employees or designated contractors representing BridgeBio. We do not conduct interviews via social media, texting tools, or other third-party sites. We never require payment or fees from job applicants. BridgeBio accepts no responsibility for any costs or charges incurred due to fraudulent activity.

Please refer to these resources offered by the FTC on how to recognize and avoid phishing scams and how to avoid job recruitment scams.
